PIZZA MENU

Pepperoni Pizza

Pepperoni is made from cured pork and beef seasoned with paprika. Pepperoni is characteristically soft, slightly smoky, and bright red in color.

Meat Deluxe

A hand-stretched crust with seasoned tomato sauce loaded with mozzarella cheese, dry cured pepperoni, crumbled Italian sausage, green and red peppers and diced onions.

Hawaiian Pizza

Hawaiian pizza is the perfect combination of sweet and savory flavors loaded on top of a crispy crust. Delicious melty mozzarella cheese locks in layers of salty bacon and juicy pineapple pieces in each slice.

Vegetable Pizza

This pizza is made with the combination of delicious vegetables like broccoli, onion, capsicum, carrot, mushroom and cauliflower along with tomatoes, pizza sauce, and cheese.
